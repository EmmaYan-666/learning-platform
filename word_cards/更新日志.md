### 家庭主题对话练习

**场景1：介绍家庭成员**
```
Child: This is my mom.
Parent: What's your mom's name?
Child: Her name is... / Mom is...
```

**场景2：表达爱意**
```
Parent: Who do you love?
Child: I love my mom and dad.
Child: I love grandma and grandpa too!
```

```
Parent: What does grandpa do?
```

## 🔄 如何添加更多家庭成员

如果需要添加其他家庭成员，可以使用以下代码：

```python
from word_list_manager import add_word

# 添加姐姐
add_word(
    word="Sister",
    chinese="姐姐",
    category="family",
    emoji="👧",
    color="#FF69B4",
    bg="FFE4E6",
    difficulty=1,
    phrases=["My sister.", "Sister is kind.", "I love sister."],
    sentences=["My sister helps me.", "Sister plays with me.", "I have a sister."]
)

# 添加哥哥
add_word(
    word="Brother",
    chinese="哥哥",
    category="boys",
    emoji="👦",
    color="#5DADE2",
    bg="EBF5FB",
    difficulty=1,
    phrases=["My brother.", "Brother is tall.", "I love brother."],
    sentences=["My brother is big.", "Brother plays soccer.", "I play with my brother."]
)
```
